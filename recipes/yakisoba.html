Yakisoba Recipe

Description

Simple and delish Japanese fried noodles reciple.

Ingredients

  • Yakisoba noodles
  • Soy sauce
  • Veggies like cabbage, carrots, and bean sprouts
  • Protein of your choosing but usually pork

Steps

  1. Fry the meat and veggies
  2. Add the noodles
  3. Add the soy sauce
